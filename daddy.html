Who's My Daddy

13LS COMEDY 1h46m

Actors: Charlie Bouguenon,Angelique Gerber,Jason Goliath

Director:Sans Moonsamy, Nadia Jaftha

When three losers Prikesh, Emma and Alfie, come together due to a weird set of circumstances they go on a wacky journey to try and find Prikesh’s long lost father. Prikesh has never known his father and after being humiliated on the TV show ‘The Joke-Off’ by comedians David Kawoo (David Kau) and Chris Bush (Chris Forrest) he realises its time to grow a pair, to find his father. Emma is a British journalist who will lose her internship in South Africa if she doesn’t get a published story…and soon..
